During the summer, a very common vacation place is the beach! There are so many beaches around the world to enjoy with your friends and family. There are many fun activites you can participate in at the beach such as surfing, parasailing, going for a boat ride and many more! There are also boardwalks on some beachere where you can enjoy many types of food and different surf shops or stores, there are also sometimes rides you can go on after a long day on the beach. Here are some necessities you should bring with you on your beach trip.

  • Sunscreen
  • Beach Chairs
  • Umbrella
  • Water
  • Snacks
