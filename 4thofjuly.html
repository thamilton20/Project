Summer: Fourth of July

Everyone looks forward to summer time! The Fourth of July is a very exciting holiday because we celebrate our Independence. Spending time
with friends and family, having barbeques, swimming, buying new bathing suits, and so much more! Seeing the beautiful fireworks while sitting around
a bonfire and roasting marshmallows to make s'mores is one of my favorite things to do on the Fourth of July.

Here are some of the many things to look forward to!

  • Fireworks
  • Barbeques
  • Swimming
  • Bonfires
  • S'mores
  • Being with friends and family
  • Lots of delicious food
  • Celebrating Independence
