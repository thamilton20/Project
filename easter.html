Easter is a a time where families gather together to have fun and celebrate. There are a lot of fun things to do, like easter egg hunts, eating a lot of food, and getting to see all of your family from all over. In easter egg hunts, you get to collect a lot of colorful eggs, filled with treats like candy or money. The night before Easter, the Easter Bunny comes to visit children, and they get baskets full of chocolate and other gifts. On Easter, there is usually a lot of food to eat, like turkey, ham, potatoes, green beans, and more. Getting to see your family is always so fun. When you haven't seen them in a long time it is nice to catch up with them, especially if they live far away to hear about their city.
